About the gym

MORE THAN A GYM

Level Up Gym in Windham, NH is built for people who are serious about results. Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time or you're a seasoned competitor, this is a place where you belong.

As a family-owned and operated gym, Roberto and Bia bring a personal touch to every interaction. Roberto — a bodybuilder, competitor, and champion — brings decades of experience to every session, taking the time to understand your goals, your history, and your limitations.

State-of-the-art equipment, a clean and private environment, and a community that genuinely cheers each other on. That's Level Up.
